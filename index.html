Educational Information

Learn about academic integrity, what constitutes misconduct, and best practices. This system educates students, teachers, and parents about examination malpractice, its consequences, and ways to promote academic integrity.

1. Understanding Examination Misconduct
Definition of Examination Misconduct:
Examination misconduct refers to any form of cheating, dishonesty, or unethical behavior during exams. It includes actions taken to gain unfair advantages or manipulate the results of an exam.

Consequences of Examination Malpractice

Understand the potential consequences of examination misconduct.

  • Academic penalties such as reduced grades
  • Suspension or expulsion from the institution
  • Permanent record notation
  • Institution Disciplinary Actions
